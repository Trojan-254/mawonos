Cowrie Shell Necklace

KES 1,200

Description

This handcrafted cowrie shell necklace features a beautiful arrangement of authentic shells collected from the Kilifi coastline. Each piece is meticulously crafted with brass accents that complement the natural beauty of the shells. The adjustable leather cord allows for a comfortable fit for all neck sizes.

Cowrie shells hold cultural significance in many Kenyan coastal communities and have been used as decorative elements for centuries. This necklace combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.
